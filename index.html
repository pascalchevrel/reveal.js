- Compiled every day with mozilla-central code - 6 first weeks of our train model - Crash reporting and telemetry on by default - Many experimental features are activated - No Firefox branding - Awesomeness++, Stability--

Why we need Firefox Nightly users?

- We need their crash reports to prioritize stability work - Telemetry can also give us valuable information - Tech-savvy crowd, report actionable bugs - If we want to ship faster we must detect regressions earlier - We will need a lot of testing with Quantum - Our core technical community, let's get them involved!

What could you do to help?

Promotion

* Promote Nightly at events, _ideally non-Mozilla events_ * Twitter, blog posts, forums: talk about our cool features, collect feedback * Translate interesting technical articles and promote them * You have a patch in Nightly? _Our blog is open for you!_

QA
